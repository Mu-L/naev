# Services

  * **shipyard**: You can buy/sell ships.
  * **bar**: Has a bar, with potential related mission unless _nomissionspawn_
    is set.
  * **missions**: Has a mission terminal.
  * **commodity**:
  * **land**: You can land. This does not include refuel and saving.
  * **nocommodities**:
  * **blackmarket**: Weapon/ship licences won't be checked here.
  * **outfits**: You can buy/sell outfits.
  * **nomissionspawn**: No missions on the mission computer / in the bar.
  * **uninhabited**: Not needed when population is 0. Disables landing
    authorization.
  * **refuel**: You can refuel. That includes saving the game.

> [!CAUTION]
> These services might be affected by special tags.
